Ingredients
-1 packet of maggi
-600ml of water
-vegetables such as carrot,beans,potato,etc(if required)
-corriander leaves(if required)

Directions
1.Pour 600ml of water into a pan and heat it till it boils.
2.Add the noodles from the packet and stir it for about half a minute.
3.Then add the masala(also the vegetables if required) into the pan and stir it continously for 3min under sim.
4. Serve Hot
